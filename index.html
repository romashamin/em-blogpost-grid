New feature in Rails 5: Render views outside of actions

Ruby on Rails has been around a long time but nevertheless continues to evolve, and the most surprising thing is that DHH is still at it — imagine such commitment to the project after 11 years!
DHH has inspired a lot of new features in Rails 5. By the way, if you want to start contributing to Rails, you can begin doing so by tracking his issues. They provide many opportunities while being very broad in complexity. For example, most recent basic ones are Enumerable#without and Enumerable#pluck.
